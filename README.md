##### Summary of Commands
* **Send GIFs**: use "02" or "002" in a sentence (e.g. `hi 02`)
* **TicTacToe**: `!2 tt`
* **Rock Paper Scissors**: `!2 rps`
* **Google something**: `!2 g your question` ( e.g. `!2 g Who is Zero Two?`)
